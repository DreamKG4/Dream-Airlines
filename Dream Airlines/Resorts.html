Bahia Principe - Jamaica

This polished all-inclusive, all-suite resort is a Victorian-style property with a Caribbean Sea beach. It's 2.1 km from Runaway Bay Golf Course and 7 km from the Green Grotto Caves. Bright suites come with canopy beds and whirlpool tubs, plus balconies or terraces. All offer pull-out sofas, minibars, satellite TV and Wi-Fi (fee). Some have sea views, and club suites add coffeemakers, a private reception and lounge access. There's 24/7 room service in club rooms. There are 5 restaurants, 6 bars and 2 kids' areas, as well as gardens with 3 lake-style pools. Other amenities include a spa, water sports and loaner bikes, plus entertainment.

Villa Del Palmar Cancun - Mexico

Set in tranquil Playa Mujeres area, this upscale resort spread across 3 modern buildings with Mayan-inspired influences is 27 km from Cancun International Airport. The property is 2.9 km from the Zona Arqueológica: El Meco and 12 km from the Paruq Urbano Kabah. Elegant rooms, suites and apartments have tiled floors and contemporary decor. Amenities include flat-screen TVs, Wi-Fi (fee) and balconies. Upgraded rooms have pull-out sofas and kitchenettes. There are 6 restaurants offering a range of cuisine from Mexican to Italian, and 5 bars. Recreation includes 5 swimming pools, a spa, a fitness center, a kids club, activities and entertainment.

Viceroy Bocas Del Toro - Panama

This tropical paradise is home to some of the purest natural beauty in Latin America: dense tropical rain forests, wide savannas, coral reefs bursting with aquatic life, and delightfully deserted beaches. The region was rated as one of the best places to travel in 2016 by Travel and Leisure. ​There is so much to see and do in the area. A paradise for thrill-chasers, nature lovers, foodies and relaxation seekers alike, Bocas del Toro is the escape you’ve been looking for. Choose one of our many tour options to enhance your stay and truly indulge in all that this vibrant area has to offer.

Sandals LaSource - Grenada

Defying every convention of traditional design, the new Sandals LaSource Grenada takes innovation beyond the realm of imagination to create an extraordinary resort unlike anything you have ever experienced. Pools in the sky and living rooms in swimming pools. Private plunge pools, cascading waterfalls and meandering river pools. Here, in the heart of Grenada's exclusive Pink Gin Beach, on an exotic island paradise where the intoxicating scents of spice and tropical blooms make a heady elixir for romance, we've taken the best of everything to create a whole new Sandals experience... one that takes you beyond the unexpected.
